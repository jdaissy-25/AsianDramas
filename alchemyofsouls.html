Alchemy of Souls S1

4.5 Stars

Alchemy of Souls is a Korean fantasy drama that follows Jang Uk, a young man from a prestigious family, who becomes entangled with Mu-deok, a blind servant girl. Unbeknownst to him, Mu-deok is actually the soul of an elite assassin named Naksu, whose soul has been trapped in Mu-deok's body due to the Alchemy of Souls. As Jang Uk trains under Mu-deok's guidance, they uncover hidden truths about their world and face challenges from powerful enemies. The first season concludes with significant revelations and a cliffhanger, setting the stage for the second season.
