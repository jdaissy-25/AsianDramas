It Is Okay to Not Be Okay

3.2 Stars

"It Is Okay to Not Be Okay" is a romantic Korean drama that centers on Moon Gang-tae, a dedicated caregiver at a psychiatric hospital, and his older brother Moon Sang-tae, who is on the autism spectrum. Their lives take a turn when they meet Ko Moon-young, a successful but traumatized children's book author with an antisocial personality disorder. Moon-young becomes obsessed with Gang-tae, leading to a complex relationship where love, trauma, and personal growth intertwine.
