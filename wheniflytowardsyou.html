When I Fly Towards You

5 Stars

“When I Fly Towards You” is a romantic Chinese drama that follows Su Zaizai, a cheerful and optimistic transfer student, who falls in love at first sight with the distant and academically gifted Zhang Lurang. Despite his outward confidence, Lurang grapples with self-doubt, largely due to constant comparisons with his younger brother, a child prodigy. His reserved nature shields him from others, until Zaizai's unwavering positivity and genuine affection begins to break through his emotional barriers.
