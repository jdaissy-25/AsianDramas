The First Frost

4 Stars

"The First Frost" is a romantic Chinese drama and a spin-off of “Hidden Love.” While Hidden Love focuses on the relationship between Sang Zhi and Duan Jiaxu, The First Frost shifts the spotlight to Sang Zhi's older brother, Sang Yan, and his own romantic journey. The story follows Sang Yan, a co-owner of a restaurant and a team lead at a video game company, who reunites with Wen Yifan, a news reporter and his high school crush. Their unexpected encounters lead to them becoming roommates after a fire damages Sang Yan's apartment. Living together helps them break down emotional barriers, and Sang Yan's caring attitude leads Wen Yifan to pursue him.
